Other Ways of Dealing with Writer's Block

How to Overcome Writer's Block

The above link describes the causes and remedies for writer's block.

  • Timing. Timing is everything. Your ideas may need some time to simply ruminate. Wait a bit before writing them down.
  • Fear. Be it fear of rejection or fear of judgment, not many are ballsy enough to put themselves out there. Don't be afraid - embrace it.
  • Being a perfectionist. The syntax, the grammar, the delivery - everything has to be on point. Sound familiar? You may be a perfectionist. The blog post gives you a resource that tells you if your work and content are good enough.

Remedies

Thankfully, there are several remedies.

  • Take a walk. Sometimes all you need is a simple physical exercise to clear the mind.
  • Eliminate distractions. Cut that gaming off, turn off social networks, and any music (unless it helps).
  • Do something to get your blood running. The endorphins running through your body will help. Some rely on running, others do weightlifting.
  • Brainstorm. Consider some things you haven't tackled yet.
  • Spend time with a loved one. Hell, maybe they could give you some food for thought!
  • Change your environment. Studies have shown that libraries and cleaning up cluttered environments such as your room are great for improving focus and concentration. Try it sometime!
